If you like this application please vote for us!

This tool has been developed in our limited spare time for the World Bank's Apps for Climate competition and your vote could help us win the "Popular Choice Award"!

How to vote:

Step 01:

Go to the Registration Page and sign up (apparently a password with upper/lower case characters and numbers is required)

Step 02:

Open the confirmation email that will arrive shortly after registration and click on the link (you might need to look for this email in your spam folder!)

Step 03:

Visit our Data Story submission page and rate it. (Attention, once clicked on the stars your vote is done, there is no confirmation or no way of going back if you make a mistake!)
